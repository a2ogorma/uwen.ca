Future of Energy Panel Discussion

UW Energy Network hosted a panel discussion on the Future of Energy. A panel of industry experts and academia shared their views on different aspects of the energy landscape.

Speakers include:

  • Lena Ahmadi, Instructor and Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University of Waterloo
  • Jeremy Goertz, Vice President, Business Development at Saturn Power
  • Dr. Roydon Fraser, Investigator in the Green Intelligent Transportation Systems and UW Professor
  • Douglas Thoms, Energy Analyst at Energent Incorporated.
