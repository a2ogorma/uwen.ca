Who are we

The University of Waterloo Energy Network is a partner of Student Energy. Together we are working to educate, inspire, and unite young leaders committed to fueling a sustainable future. We are a group of students bound together by a shared belief in a sustainable approach to the Energy Industry and Climate Change. UWEN offers the chance for like-minded students to attend educational seminars and panel discussions, participate in exciting competitions and network with industry professionals. We endeavor to provide a platform for all points of view with the aim of stimulating discussion and broadening knowledge.
